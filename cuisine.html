Cuisine

  1. Soup Jomou
    2. Soup Jomou is a very popular dish in the Haitian culture because it represents Haiti's independence from French colonial rule on January 1st, 1804.During slavery, only the French masters had the opprotunity to enjoy such delicacy, which was prepared by the slaves. So, it has been a long tradition to eat soup joumou on the 1st of January to symbolize Haiti's freedom and dignity. This dish consist of butternut or acorn squash, root vegetables like parsnips and carrots,beef and sometimes pasta.
