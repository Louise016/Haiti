Labadee
Labadee is a beach restort owned by port by the Royal Caribbean International cruise company.This spot is very popular among cruise lovers.There's many fun actitivities you can do there, which includes ziplining, parasailing, and snorkeling.This reseort is located in Nord, Haiti.

Citadelle Laferrière
The Citadelle Laferrière was a mountaintop fortress built to protect Haiti from the French invasion, and today this monument symbolizes Haiti's independence and strenght. This fortress has a magnificent view and it is surrounded by green field. The monument is located on the Bonnet à l'Evêque mountaintop in Nord, Haiti.

Saut-Mathurine
Located in the Southwest region of Haiti, Saut-Mathurine is the largest waterfall in the area. A lot of people go to this area to see the beautiful view, swimming, ziplining and padding.
